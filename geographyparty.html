Geography Party Deluxe

Group Project Semester 1 - BTSGP - Made in 1 Week

Context

Geography Party Deluxe is a Mutliplayer Trivia Party Game where you need to traverse Luxembourg and collect as many "Kachk√©isschmier" (basically a slice of bread with a special luxembourgish cheese on it) as you can!

This game was made in a group of 4 students in an event that the BTSGP calls the "Power Week", where we have one week to make a game in a selected theme. The theme for this game was "Educational Game - Geography".

Have a look at the trailer if you're interested:

My Involvement

For this project I was in charge of programming the Overworld (the Board). I made sure that the Players could move on the board and make their selections.

Learning Outcome

As this was one of the earlier projects, team coordination was one of the key things that I learned. I also learned how to make cinematic camera transitions.

Challenges

The biggest challenge in this game for me was giving appropriate feedback and making the characters visually traverse on the grid. Another problem was having multiple characters stand on the same tile which was a bit tricky to handle.
