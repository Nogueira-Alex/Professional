Dwarf Miner

MemoMoti 2020, made within 4 Weeks

  • Click Here to Play!

Context

In Dwarf Miner you help Ram the dwarf traverse a dangerous mine! Slot the right bridge parts into the broken bridges to help Ram securely get through the mine!

This game was made in a group of 4 interns supervised by my boss, the Owner of MemoMoti : Gira Szakmar and the Technical lead : Farzam Molk Ara.

Dwarf Miner is an educational Game made with the MemoMoti framework for EduGamiTec.

My Involvement

For this project I was the lead programmer and sound designer, just as in Driftonaut I designed the software architecture for the game and programmed in collaboration with another intern everything in the game from the UI to input handling to the gameplay feedback. The game was made in Unity.

Learning Outcomes

As this was the first of the 2 (and a half) games that we made at MemoMoti, it was also the first software architecture I had to design in a professional environment. The software architecture certainly wasn't perfect, but it proved to work. But the best thing is that I now learned out of my mistakes and know how to design an even better one!

Challenges

This game proved being quite affine to bugs due to most of it being physics driven, reacting to collisions. I also was in charge of making the Camera System which you can see in the gif.
